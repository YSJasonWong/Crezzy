About CREZZY

CREEZY stand for Car Rental Easy is one of the largest car rental organisations in Hong Kong. We serve more than 40 million customers through car rental services. Our network covers 65 countries and territories in Europe, Asia, the Middle East and Africa, North America and Latin America. Hong Kong is our homeland.

We aim to be where the growth is, connecting customers to opportunities, enabling them to thrive and economies to prosper, and ultimately helping people to fulfil their hopes and realise their ambitions.
